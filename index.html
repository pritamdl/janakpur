  • Sitla Sthan Temple

    Sitla Sthan Temple is a Hindu temple, located in the heart of Janakpur.The temple was renovated on 4 March 2023.There are statues of Maa Shitala and Maa Kali in this temple. It is hundreds of years old monument.

  • Hanuman Mandir

    This temple has a great charming statue of Lord Hanuman. It is located at the center of the village. Height of this temple is approx 52 feets.There are 3 floors in the temple which has Lord Hanuman's statue at ground floor; on first ,second & third floor Lord Rama , Lord krishna & lord shiva's shivling statues construction are in progress.

  • Shiwalaya

    Shiwalaya is very famous and highly worshiped temple of the village . It is situated at the entry point of the village. the temple was established on 8 march 2008.Lord Shiva & Goddess Parvati are worshiped in this temple.

  • Our School

    Middle School Janakpur is a co-educational school located at the centre of the village.The school is run by the Bihar Government. The Students who studies in this school archive higher post in their carrier.

  • Jakhraj Baba Sthan

    Jakhraj Baba sthan is situated in the field. The Peoples of the village believe that the god protects the crop from naural disaster.
